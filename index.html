Our Work Process

We only need the name, address and phone number of the seller along with the Make and Model of car.

We contact the seller to let him know that you have retained our services and that our nearest inspector will be calling him shortly to arrange a convenient time to inspect the car.

When the completed report is ready, we send you an e-mail to let you know that you can view it on our web site.

We also provide a phone number for you to call us if you have any questions.

That's it! - The whole thing is usually accomplished in 24 hours.

Our detailed reports cover an extensive amount of information, digital photos & pricing guidance.
